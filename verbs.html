Words which tell us about the action people or animal do. Such action words are called verbs.

What is verb tense?

A verb tense tells us when the action happened - an action or past state of being (i.e. a to be verb)

Simple Past Tense :

Past tense means something that already happend or a past state of being.

How do you make a verb past tense?

For most verbs,you add the ending -D or -ED.
